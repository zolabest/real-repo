Popular Cities in Tigray

  • Mekelle (መቐለ) - The capital city of the Tigray region
  • Axum (ኣኽሱም) - Known for its obelisks and archaeological sites
  • Adigrat (ኣድግራት) - An important commercial and industrial center
  • Shire (Inda Selassie) ሽሬ (ኢንዳ ሰላሴ) - Known for nearby archaeological sites
  • Humera (ሑመራ) - Situated in the westernmost part of Tigray
  • Adwa (ኣድዋ) - Famous for the Battle of Adwa in 1896
