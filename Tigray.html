Popular Cities in Tigray, Ethiopia

Mekele

Mekele is the capital city of the Tigray Region. It is known for its rich history, including landmarks such as the Palace of Emperor Yohannes IV and the Martyrs' Memorial Monument. The city also serves as a cultural and economic hub for the region.

Axum

Axum is one of the oldest continuously inhabited places in Africa and is famous for its ancient ruins, including the obelisks, the Church of St. Mary of Zion, and the Queen of Sheba's Palace. It is considered one of Ethiopia's holiest cities.

Adigrat

Adigrat is a vibrant city located in the eastern part of Tigray. It is known for its diverse culture, bustling markets, and picturesque landscapes. Visitors can explore historical sites such as the Debre Damo Monastery and the Mariam Dearit Rock Church.
